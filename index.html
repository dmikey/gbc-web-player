How to Install on iOS

Follow these steps to add a The Rad Boy to your home screen:

  1. Tap the Share button (a square with an arrow pointing upward) at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Scroll down and select Add to Home Screen.
  3. Customize the app's name if desired, then tap Add.

The Rad Boy will now appear on your home screen, ready to use!
